Overview

Print
The pan-European component is coordinated by the European Environment Agency and will produce high resolution data sets describing 5 land cover types: artificial (man-made) surfaces, forests, grasslands, wetlands, and small water bodies. The pan-European component is also updating the Corine Land Cover dataset to the reference year 2012.
